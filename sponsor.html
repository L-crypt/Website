You can make a difference so why dont you ?

BloodConnect is a completely non-profit organisation. To keep our services running 24x7, we need your support. With every ₹ 50 donated, we are able to save 1 life

Your Donation Enables Us To

  • Arrange a cab for a donor who has to go to AIIMS to save someone in desperate need
  • Motivate an 18 year old to donate blood for the first time on his / her birthday via a street play
  • Convert a one time donor to a regular life saver by bringing happiness to his face with a small goodie post donation
  • Recruit the best volunteers from colleges like SRCC, IIT Delhi to work for this cause and make an impact
  • Mentor the team by having self made entrepreneurs and people from corporates like Reliance, TCS, Bain & Co, Shell join us
  • Build a movement amongst the youth to donate blood and save lives
  • Save lives. Everything we do: starts and ends with it- we work to save lives

How can you donate ?

All Indian contributions made to BloodConnect are eligible for tax deduction of 50% or 100% [based on our affiliation] u/s 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. We can provide a copy of 12A / 80G certificates as per requirement
