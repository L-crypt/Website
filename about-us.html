Our Vision

To solve the problem of blood shortage in India in our lifetime

BloodConnect has developed a 360 degree solution to the problem of blood shortage.

What Makes Us Different ?

Organizing Blood Donation Camps

We organize blood donation camps in various cities with various government blood banks. We believe that organizing camps is the fastest way to solving the problem of blood shortage. They are safe, and give you a very easy way out to donate blood (saves a trip to the hospital).

College Partners

Being largely student volunteer driven, colleges are the backbone of our organization. With our college partners, we are able to organize seamless blood donation camps throughout the year, recruit the most motivated, sharp and hardworking volunteers and last, but most importantly, motivate the newly 18, the first time donors to become regular lifesavers.
